🤖 Real-Time Hand Sign Detection

This AI-powered app detects hand signs from your webcam feed and estimates the distance of your hand from the camera.

  • 🖐️ Recognizes universal hand signs like Peace, Fist, Thumbs Up, and more
  • 📏 Estimates hand-to-camera distance in real-time
  • 🎯 Powered by OpenCV, MediaPipe, and a trained ML model
  • ⚡ Deployed with Streamlit for instant accessibility
